How our Technology Works

Our revolutionary eyewear uses cutting-edge technology to help restore visual perception for blind individuals through multiple advanced mechanisms.

Image Capture & Processing

High-resolution cameras embedded in the frames capture real-time visual information. Our advanced AI processes these images into patterns that can be interpreted by the brain.

Neural Stimulation

Micro-electrodes in the frames send precise, non-invasive signals to the optic nerve pathways. This bypasses damaged retinal cells and delivers visual information directly to the brain.

Brain Adaptation

Our technology works with the brain's natural neuroplasticity. Through regular use, the brain learns to interpret these signals, gradually improving visual perception over time.

Personalized Calibration

Each device is custom-calibrated to the individual's specific condition and visual system. Our specialists work with each patient to optimize the device for their unique needs.
